What is Lorem Ipsum?

"Lorem ipsum" is a scrambled, non-sensical Latin text commonly used as placeholder text in the printing and typesetting industry. It has been the industry's standard dummy text since the 1500s.

The standard passage starts with: "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua..."

Why Use Placeholder Text?

1. Focus on Design

The main purpose is to prevent the viewer directly reading the content. If you use real text (like "This is a header"), the client might focus on the copy ("That header is wrong!") rather than the visual design choices (font, color, spacing).

2. Realistic Distribution

Lorem Ipsum has a more-or-less normal distribution of letters, as opposed to using "Content here, content here", making it look like readable English.

Historical Origin

Contrary to popular belief, it is not random gibberish. It has roots in a piece of classical Latin literature from 45 BC, making it over 2000 years old. Richard McClintock, a Latin professor, traced the word "consectetur" to a passage in "De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" (The Extremes of Good and Evil) by Cicero.
