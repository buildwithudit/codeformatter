Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026
The "Plain English" Promise

We do not want your data. Everything you paste here (JSON, Code, Text) stays on your device. It is processed by your browser's CPU and never transmitted to our servers.

1. How We Process Data

Code Formatter is a Client-Side Application. This is the safest architecture for developer tools because:

Local Processing

Calculations happen on your machine. No API calls send your code out.

No Logs

Since we don't have a backend database for input, we literally cannot log your data.

2. Cookies & Local Storage

We use zero tracking pixels. However, to improve your UI experience, we use your browser's Local Storage for:

  • Theme Preference: Remembering if you like Dark Mode.
  • Tool Selection: Keeping your last used tool active.

3. Contact & Security

If you have any concerns about the security implementation of this site, you can reach out directly to the developer, Udit Sharma.
